Gulfshore Funding provides business-to-business financing through revenue-based structures designed to support operating companies. Our financing is provided through the purchase of future receivables and is not a loan.

How It Works

  1. Submit a business financing application.
  2. We review your business information and revenue history.
  3. If approved, you receive an offer outlining the purchased receivables, factor rate, and revenue percentage.
  4. After acceptance and ACH authorization, funds are disbursed.
  5. Repayment occurs automatically through ACH debits tied to business revenue.
